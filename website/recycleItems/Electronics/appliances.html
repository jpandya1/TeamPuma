Appliances



Appliances can be separated into 2 categories. All appliances should be unplugged and cooled down before recycling. For small appliances, any non-metal parts of the appliance that can be separated should be before recycling. For large appliances, donating, or getting the manufactor to pick the appliance up are the best recycling options.
